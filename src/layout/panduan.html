How it Works

1

Capture image

Go to the upload page, click on the "Take a Picture" button. This allows you to do one of two things: take the photo from your phone or select the image from your files. An alternative would be to drag and drop the image there. Use high quality image for accurate results.

2

AI-Powered Disease Detection

Wait for the loading icon to disappear. This indicates that our AI is analyzing your image. Once the analysis is complete, you will see the name of the disease detected, a short description and links to articles that provide more information about the disease.

3

Personalized treatment recommendations

You will receive personalized treatment recommendations and a list of nearby stores selling the products, along with experts you can consult for more help.
